About Us


wecareforyou Welfare Organization runs a shelter home for the old and destitute in the  Sangam Vihar, New Delhi. We provide free food, shelter, medical services and recreational facilities to the aged and the poor found on streets of Sangam Vihar and surrounding areas. Currently, there are 140 residents and some of them have been recommended by the police, while others were dropped off by their children.
Over their 3 years of operations, wecareforyou realised that the section of the society which needs the most help are the old and the abandoned peoples. There is a need for a place where these citizens receive love, respect and dignity in the last stages of their lives. Hence, they plan to set-up a chain of free abandoned citizens homes. Their goal is to provide a healthy and friendly environment to abandoned citizens. The facility will be inclusive of nutritious meals, recreation and medical facilities for the elderly.
